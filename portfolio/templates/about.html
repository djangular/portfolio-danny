DANNY NIETO

A fashion photographer based out of Paris, who loves to click, cook & travel.

Who am I?

My name’s Daniel, but call me Danny.

I’m a photographer from LA living in Boston. I go to school for photojournalism and spend most weekends bartending.

There will always be a place in my heart for black & white street photography, but I’m comfortable shooting in all styles.

From this, I’ve noticed my interests and skills gravitate towards nightlife, music, and portrait photography, for I feel most at home in these environments.

What am I doing?

In Spanish, the word “Duende” doesn’t quite translate over to English. Yet, it's understood as this ineffable force that moves people to empathize; it’s the soul we search for in the people, places and things we encounter.

And that's the main concept behind Duende in RAW; I’m looking for the beauty in peoples' individuality.

I take pictures to separate the intimate from the stoic, to showcase the individual from the masses, and to capture “El Duende” of the human condition.
