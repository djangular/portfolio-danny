About Me

Who am I?

Hi, my name’s Daniel but call me Danny. I’m a photographer from Los Angeles living in Boston. I’m going to school for photojournalism and spend most weekends bartending. I like all sorts of photography but gravitate towards the streets, nightlife, and black and white.

What am I doing?

In Spanish, the word “duende” doesn’t quite translate into English. It's understood as the ineffable force that moves people to empathize; it’s the soul we search for in the people, places and things we come across. That's the main concept behind Duende in RAW. I’m looking for the beauty in peoples' vulnerability and individuality. I take pictures to showcase the individual, to separate the intimate from the stoic, and to capture the duende of the human condition.
